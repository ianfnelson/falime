Day Four - Rest Day

More rain. So we sat in Braemar Youth Hostel, and wrote postcards, read the newspapers, but most of all just watched TV. This Morning, The Labour Party Conference, Fifteen-To-One, Countdown... it was just like being a student again. Richard and Judy are still icons of daytime TV, Blair still doesn't use verbs (112 syntactical orphans in a single speech!), and Richard Whitely still makes the most piss-poor jokes this side of the Zoe Ball Breakfast Show.
