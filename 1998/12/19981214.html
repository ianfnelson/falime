Monday, 14th December 1998


John of the Cross
Public Holiday, New Zealand


Y2K-383


Up at 5:30am and off for a final full week of work before the holidays. Hardly any colleagues around, they all seemed to be on courses, holiday, or sick. So I actually got some work done. Watched some bad TV, went to bed.

I'd like to say more, but there's nothing to say.
