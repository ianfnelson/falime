I like reading online journals. Most days, I enjoy reading them more than I enjoy writing my own. Maybe you noticed that! In fact, the very reason I write a journal is because I enjoy reading others so much. So, without further ado, here are a few of the sites I check out most regularly:


An Exercise Great images, and thoughtful bite-size paragraphs. I always look forward to my daily visit to Atlanta!
Coredumps From My Brain I harbour a secret desire to be as geeky as Andrew. But he has a head start on me - I don't live in Seattle.
Cyberpunk Musings Fun, fresh, sassy, and regularly updated. Lots of great cultural reference points, too.
Gracefully Yours The anonymity of this journal thankfully doesn't detract from it's excellence. Always something interesting to read here.
Lifestyle Tips For The Dead Genre-defining. A mighty-fine read.
Randomly Ever After Surely you already read The Gus? But.. you must do?!
